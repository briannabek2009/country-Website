Dominican Republic

The Dominican Republic has beautiful beaches and tall mountains. The beaches have soft sand and clear blue water, perfect for swimming and relaxing. The mountains are green and peaceful, with great views and fresh air. Together, the beaches and mountains make the country a wonderful place to visit.

Dominicans are known for their lively music, fun dances, and tasty drinks. People love to dance merengue and bachata, which are two popular styles of music in the country.
