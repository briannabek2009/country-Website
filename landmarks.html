Historical Landmarks:

Alcázar de Colón – Santo Domingo The former home of Diego Columbus (Christopher Columbus’s son).

Fortaleza Ozama:The oldest military fortress in the Americas, built in the early 16th century.

Puerta del Conde: A historic gate symbolizing Dominican independence.

Nature landmarks:

Salto el limon-samana: A beautiful water fall, with clear blue waters.

Playa macao: A nice beach with soft sand and crystal clear blue water.
